# Vändtia Game Rules

## Setup

- Standard 52-card deck.
- 2 players (Human vs Bot for MVP).
- Each player gets:
  - 3 face-down cards in a row.
  - 3 face-up cards, one on top of each face-down card.
  - 3 hand cards.
- Remaining cards form the stock (draw pile).

## Rearrangement Phase

- Before play starts, players can swap any card in their hand with any of their face-up cards (1-for-1).
- Players press "Finish" when done.
- Hands are hidden during this phase. Face-up cards are revealed to the opponent only after both are "Ready".

## Turn Order

- Play proceeds clockwise (alternating in 2-player).
- Player left of the dealer starts. Dealer is chosen randomly for the first game.

## Playing Cards

- Play one or multiple cards of the same rank.
- Must be equal to or higher than the current top rank of the play pile.
- If the play pile is empty, any card/set can be played.
- **Special Card: 2** - Can be played on anything. The player gets another turn (the pile is not burned).
- **Special Card: 10** - Can be played on anything. Burns the pile and gives the player another turn.
- **Four of a kind** - If the top of the pile has four cards of the same rank (played at once or accumulated), the pile is burned and the player gets another turn.

## Drawing

- After playing, if a player has < 3 cards in hand, they draw from stock until they have 3 or stock is empty.
- Drawing happens immediately after each play, including plays of a 2 or 10 that grant another turn (so the player continues their turn with a refilled hand).

## Picking Up

- If a player cannot or chooses not to play, they pick up the entire play pile into their hand. Turn ends.
- The next player starts a new empty pile.

## Chance Card

- If a player has no valid move, instead of picking up the play pile they may take a "chance" card: the top card of the stock.
- If the chance card can be played, it goes straight onto the play pile and the turn continues as a normal play (special cards still apply).
- If the chance card cannot be played, the player picks up the play pile **and** the chance card into their hand. Turn ends.
- A chance card is only available while the stock is not empty.

## Endgame

- When stock is empty, players stop drawing.
- Hand cards must be exhausted before using face-up cards.
- When a player's hand and the stock are both empty, the player takes one of their three face-up cards into their hand and plays it from there. This repeats — one card at a time — each time the hand empties, until all face-up cards are used.
- All three face-up cards must be used before playing face-down cards.
- When playing a face-down card, the player chooses one and flips it.
  - If it's a valid move, it's played. If it also allows another move (e.g., 10 or 2), they continue if possible.
  - If it's NOT a valid move, the player picks up the pile AND the flipped card into their hand.
- A player is out when they have no cards left.
- First player to finish wins (in 2-player).
